My Journey

Welcome! I’m Sara Hashemi Nasab — an entrepreneurial spirit, lifelong learner, and lover of meaningful connections. I’m passionate about blending knowledge, creativity, and nature to create experiences that inspire growth and authenticity.

My journey bridges many worlds — from entrepreneurship and emotional intelligence to art, design, and wellness. I’m endlessly curious about how science and self-awareness can help us live with more clarity, compassion, and creativity.

Beyond business and research, I find joy in nature walks, dance, and art — spaces where I can reflect, recharge, and express myself freely. Whether designing clothes, painting, or simply sharing ideas, I see creativity as a way to connect deeply with others and the world around us.

This website is more than my personal corner of the web — it’s a celebration of collaboration, exploration, and growth. I’m honored to share it with the wonderful people who make every project meaningful.

My Vision

I envision a world where leaders lead with empathy, products are crafted with consciousness, and communities thrive through authentic connection with nature and one another. Through Wild Connections, I’m committed to turning this vision into reality — one mindful conversation, one natural product, one transformative experience at a time.

Alongside creating natural skincare that nurtures both skin and soul, I also share psychological teachings and leadership insights that help others grow with confidence, compassion, and purpose. Together, we explore how wellness and wisdom can shape the way we live and lead.
